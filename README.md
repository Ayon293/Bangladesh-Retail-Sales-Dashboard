# Bangladesh-Retail-Sales-Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from Bangladesh between 2021 and 2023 using Power BI.
The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, revenue trends, city-wise performance, category-wise sales, seasonal patterns, and business growth opportunities.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Chittagong Leads Sales

Chittagong generated the highest revenue, outperforming Dhaka and indicating strong retail activity outside the capital city.

### 2. Groceries is the Top Category

Groceries consistently achieved the highest sales across all years.

### 3. Seasonal Sales Peak

Sales peaked during April–May, likely due to Eid-related shopping activities.

### 4. Weekday Sales Dominate

Weekday sales were significantly higher than weekend sales.

### 5. Discounts Have Minimal Impact

Sales showed almost no correlation with discounts, suggesting that discount strategies alone may not drive business growth.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory before Eid seasons.
* Focus on expansion in high-performing cities.
* Prioritize grocery products due to stable demand.
* Improve customer loyalty and retention strategies.
* Use data-driven planning for future sales growth.
